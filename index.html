About Us

Jay Z Build & Design is a full-service design and development firm based in the Greater Los Angeles area, specializing in creating sophisticated, functional, and timeless living spaces. We are committed to delivering homes that seamlessly blend aesthetics with practicality, ensuring they not only reflect our clients' unique lifestyles but also enhance their everyday living experience. By carefully considering their needs, family dynamics, and long-term vision, we craft personalized spaces that stand the test of time. Our expertise spans architectural and interior design, material selection, construction management, and ADU development, providing a seamless experience from concept to completion. Whether designing a primary residence or an ADU, we ensure each space is thoughtfully planned and built to maximize functionality, comfort, and value. Our founder, Jay Zhao, holds a Master’s Degree in Architecture from the University of Westminster and brings over 20 years of international experience across China, the UK, and the US. With a strong foundation in both modern design principles and traditional craftsmanship, Jay leads a team dedicated to more than just constructing buildings—we create spaces that enhance the way people live. At Jay Z Build & Design, we turn ideas into reality, designing and building homes that are as enduring as they are beautiful.
